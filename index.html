About

I am currently working as a Researcher & Software Engineer at Brain Innovation (Maastricht, The Netherlands). I have received my PhD degree in 2020 from at Maastricht University, Cognitive Neuroscience Department. During my PhD, I have imaged brain stem as well as cortical regions with anatomical, functional and diffusion MRI in living humans. Other than recording and analyzing brain visuals, I was also developing image processing methods specifically designed to address the challenges of ultra high field MRI scanners. My ORCID is 0000-0001-7761-3727.
