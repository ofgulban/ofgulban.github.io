Omer Faruk Gulban

I am a PhD student working on mapping the human auditory pathway using resonance imaging (MRI) at ultra-high fields (7 Tesla and above). This involves imaging brain stem as well as cortical regions with anatomical, functional and diffusion MRI in living humans. Other than recording and analyzing brain images, I am also developing image processing methods specifically designed to address the challenges of 7 and 9.4 Tesla scanners at Maastricht University.
